purpose

As we have previously explained in the cover letter We are Accessibility Feedback, a nonprofit company that is made up of blind individuals who are passionate about accessibility, which includes web accessibility. We are writing concerning the accessibility of your 3CX software. We would like to focus on the IOS app and web client with Google Chrome “3CX Click To Call” extension installed on the Google Chrome browser. The extension is in fact very useful in many regards and does provide great value. However, if it is not accessible, it is difficult for blind people to use so is able to provide less value and usability for blind people. In this way the blind person won’t be able to experience for themselves the great benefits of the 3CX programs and will be at a disadvantage. The Blind user would use screen readers, to access 3CX software which includes JAWS For Windows screen reader (JAWS), NVDA for windows screen reader (NVDA), Voiceover for Mac OS screen reader (VoiceOver), and Voiceover for iOS screen reader (Voiceover), in order to perform their work tasks and we have discovered the ap

We have built this site to host our issues that we tracked and the screen shots that we took in regards to those Issues.

Please feel free to explore our site. Please do not hesitate to ask questions if you do not understand, chime in if you know of or can find other accessibility issues, or to make comments in general.
