purpose

As we have previously explained in the cover letter We at RD Partners INC have employees, consultants, contractors, and interns who are blind . We would like to write to share our experience with the community using 3cx products as a company that employs blind individuals who use screen readers, which Includes JAWS For Windows screen reader (JAWS), NVDA for windows screen reader (NVDA), Voiceover for Mac OS screen reader (VoiceOver), and Voiceover for iOS screen reader (Voiceover). We have had some difficulty with using 3cx sometimes it is even impossible to access certain features .

We have built this site to host our issues that we tracked and the screen shots that we took in regards to those Issues.

Please feel free to explore our site. Please do not hesitate to ask questions if you do not understand, chime in if you know of or can find other accessibility issues, or to make comments in general.
